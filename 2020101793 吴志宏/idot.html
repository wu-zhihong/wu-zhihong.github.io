My Idot--Russell Westbrook

Russell Westbrook (born November 12, 1988 in Long Beach, CA) is an American professional basketball player and point guard who plays for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association.

Russell Westbrook entered the NBA through the draft in 2008 and has played for the Thunder, Rockets, Wizards and Lakers. He was named First Team All-Rookie in his rookie season and was the MVP in 2016-17. He was selected to the All-Star team for 10 times, and was named MVP of the All-Star Game in 2015 and 2016. Two-time All-NBA first team, five-time All-NBA second team, two-time All-NBA Third team; He averaged a triple-double in 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2020-21; In 2020-21, he became the NBA's all-time triple-double champion. In October 2021, he was selected as one of the 75 biggest stars.
